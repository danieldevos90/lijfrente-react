It's easy and non-binding to apply for a loan offer. There are 3 easy steps:

- Submit your primary company details and personal information via the form on our homepage.
- Additional information can be sent to speed up the handling of your application.
- Are we able to give you a loan offer and do you agree with the costs and terms? Upon approval viaiDIN we will pay out the loan the same day.
