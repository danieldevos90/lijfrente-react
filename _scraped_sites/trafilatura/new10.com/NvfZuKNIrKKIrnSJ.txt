# "The power of 10"

**
"When Dutch giant ABN Amro launched New10, its Cloud-based funding spin-off for SMEs in 2017, it turned traditional business banking on its head." - Fintech Finance**

If 10 is a perfect score, ABN Amro has set the bar high for its latest venture. Launched to service Dutch SMEs with business loans - and especially the new wave of digital-savvy entrepreneurs - New10 was conceived, constructed and launched following a group wide strategy review at the bank in 2016.
